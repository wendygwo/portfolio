About Me

I'm a Los Angeles-based web developer with 7 years of experience in electrical and systems engineering and engineering management. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive (WDI) bootcamp and have an affinity for Ruby on Rails and Javascript.

I'm passionate about technical products and their ability to make all aspects of consumers' lives easier and more efficient and am looking for opportunities to work with like-minded startups and individuals.

In my free time, I love being active and being outdoors. The personal achievements I'm proudest of are successfully summiting Mt. Kilimanjaro and completing a full marathon, the LA Marathon. When it's too cold or too dark to be outdoors, you can find me playing in co-ed dodgeball leagues throughout the LA area!

I also love traveling internationally. My one new year's resolution each year is to visit another country and I've luckily been able to keep that up the last few years!

Where My Dogs At?

This project is an app that simplifies the lives of dog owners, especially households where multiple people care for the dog. The site gives users a centralized location where owners can check off caretaking actions, such as 'Fed breakfast?' for each dog in their list. Users can also add additional owners, for each dog they own, so that all concerned parties will see the latest updates for the dog.

Technologies:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • Ruby gems:
    • Paperclip, Figaro, Bcrypt, Letter Opener
  • PostgreSQL
  • Heroku
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Twitter bootstrap
